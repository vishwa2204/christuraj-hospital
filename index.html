Appointments are encouraged to ensure timely consultation with our healthcare providers. Walk-ins are accepted for emergencies, but appointments help minimize waiting times.

Christuraj Hospital provides a range of medical services including outpatient care, inpatient care, surgical procedures, diagnostic imaging, laboratory services, and specialized treatments in various medical specialties.

Visiting hours may vary depending on the ward and patient condition. Generally, visiting hours are between 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM, but it's recommended to confirm with the hospital for specific timings.

Appointments can be made by calling the hospital directly or visiting the hospital in person. Some departments may also offer online appointment booking facilities.
